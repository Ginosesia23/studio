# **App Name**: Elevate Tech

## Core Features:

- Service Overview Display: Showcase core hosting and technical support services with a clear value proposition.
- Detailed Service Sections: Provide dedicated sections with in-depth descriptions for hosting solutions and technical support offerings.
- AI-Powered Service Recommendation Tool: A tool that guides potential clients through a series of questions to recommend the most suitable hosting or support package, capturing user input for follow-up.
- About Us & Mission Statement: Informative section detailing company background, expertise, and commitment to client success.
- Client Inquiry Form: A simple contact form for potential clients to submit questions or request a consultation.
- Responsive Design: Ensure the landing page is fully functional and visually appealing across all device types and screen sizes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, trustworthy blue (#29427A), conveying professionalism and stability.
- Background color: A subtle, cool off-white (#EEF3F7), providing a clean and spacious feel, visually linked to the primary blue.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan (#24C5DB), used for call-to-action buttons and key highlights to draw attention without being overwhelming.
- Headlines font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-savvy, and engaging feel. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent readability and a neutral, professional appearance in longer content.
- Use sleek, minimalist line icons to represent services and features, maintaining a professional and contemporary aesthetic.
- Clean, modular layout with generous whitespace to enhance readability and guide the user's eye through the service offerings efficiently. Utilize sticky navigation for easy access to different sections.
- Subtle fade-in and slide-up animations for content sections as they enter the viewport, adding a refined touch without distracting from information.
